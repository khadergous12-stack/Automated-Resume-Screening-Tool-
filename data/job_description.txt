JOB TITLE: Data Analyst

COMPANY: TechCorp Analytics

LOCATION: New York, NY (Hybrid)

ABOUT THE ROLE:
We are looking for a skilled Data Analyst to join our Business Intelligence team.
You will be responsible for analyzing large datasets, creating dashboards, and providing
actionable insights to support business decisions.

REQUIRED SKILLS (Must Have):
- SQL (Advanced: complex queries, joins, stored procedures)
- Python (Data analysis: Pandas, NumPy)
- Excel (Advanced: pivot tables, macros, VBA)
- Power BI (Dashboard creation and DAX formulas)
- Statistical analysis and data visualization

NICE TO HAVE:
- Tableau
- R programming
- Machine Learning basics
- Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- ETL pipeline experience

EXPERIENCE REQUIRED:
- Minimum 2 years of experience as a Data Analyst
- Experience with large datasets (1M+ rows)
- Experience presenting insights to non-technical stakeholders

EDUCATION:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field

RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Build and maintain Power BI dashboards for executive reporting
- Write complex SQL queries to extract and transform data
- Automate reporting using Python scripts
- Perform statistical analysis to identify trends and patterns
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather data requirements
- Present findings to stakeholders clearly and concisely
